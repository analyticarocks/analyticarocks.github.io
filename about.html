Meet b2match!

What makes us unique is our valuable expertise on how to organize successful business matchmaking events.

We are on a mission!

b2match can look back on over 12 years of experience in organizing matchmaking events and we think of ourselves as the experts in the field of business matchmaking.

On account of our long-time expertise we have come to understand how event networking must be managed to lessen the burden of the organiser, while maximizing the outcome for the event participants.

Our goal is to make this expertise accessible for you and your participants. We do that by offering you the best tool out there when it comes to professional business matchmaking, but also by being your point of contact and your associate in event organisation. We are not only a software provider, but a proactive and innovative partner in making your event a success.

Our locations

b2match operates from its 2 headquarters in Vienna and Zagreb, and currently employs 14 team members. From development to support, everything at b2match is done in-house, to ensure our product and the close contact with our customers lives up to the high quality standards we set ourselves. We are constantly striving to advance the tool, improve the b2match platform and to replace complexity with simplicity.
