Use Cases

Different types of networking events organized with b2match.

Virtual Meetings/Online Event

Virtual meetings can be incorporated into events to make online participation possible and offer a valuable extension for meeting formats at events. So participants who cannot visit an event physically (e.g. Coronavirus) can take part remotely and meet other participants through our video conferencing tool. This can be used to host hybrid events (physical-online), where organizers choose to offer virtual meetings just as an extra format or to run their event entirely online.
Mobile World Congress 2020
Bercelona, Spain
Visit Website
Due to the COVID-19, the organizers of the Mobile World Congress decided to cancel this year’s event but to keep on with the networking part. They went for a hybrid solution and used virtual meetings as an extension for their physical event, making participation for everyone possible who could not travel to Barcelona because of travel restrictions.
